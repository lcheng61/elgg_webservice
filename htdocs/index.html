LoveBeauty is a marketplace that helps beauty lovers become more beautiful by learning make-up tips and trending fashion products.

Shopping with fingertip

@Buyer: With the mobile app, we are creating a transformational shopping experience by providing a digital shopping mall directly to tens of millions of consumers. It is also a social platform for beauticians and salons to share experiences, to network with each other and to follow fashion savvy insiders.

@Seller: Love Beauty is helping online beauty product/service providers promote their business and find potential customers. It enables any existing Amazon.com, eBay, or independent online merchants to reach a very relevant set of qualified consumers for their products and to generate a high volume of sales within days or even hours after creating their merchant accounts.

Unique Experience

When shopping for fashion, "I'll know when I see it". Our app will provide beauty ideas, community, and products in one place. This boosts the consumer's knowledge and confidence in what they are purchasing. The contents, combined with the mobile devices' portability bleeds into moments that consumers might not have otherwise shopped.

Intimate Community

Due to the social features of our app, people will feel really connected because sellers are real people rather than business names only. It can help us build a closer community and increase the stickiness of the users compared with traditional online retailers.

Better Marketplace

As a seller, you will know who like or purchase your products, and who comment on your products. You can follow them on our platform to learn what the most attractive products are to consumers. More importantly, if your products are attractive and have good quality, beauty lovers will FOLLOW YOU!
